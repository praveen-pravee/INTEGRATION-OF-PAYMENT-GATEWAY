ENVIRONMENT PROTECTION TEAM




We live in a beautiful world. Planet Earth has an astonishing variety of life and stunning natural habitats. Unfortunately, the actions of humans on this planet has caused damage to this beautiful world. An over dependence on fossil fuels has contributed significantly to the greenhouse effect and climate change deforestation has destroyed the natural habitats of species all over the world poachers have led to the near extinction of a number of beautiful species and plastic pollution is choking up oceans around the globe. Thankfully, there a number of non-profit environmental organisations that work tirelessly to protect and conserve our planet, campaigning and taking action against pressing issues and we are one of those organizations who aimed to conserve this green planet at any cost.
